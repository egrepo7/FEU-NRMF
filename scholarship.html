Scholarship Program / Awards

Started in June 2013, the International Society continue to encourage students of the FEU-NRMF School of Medical Technology to excel by providing scholarship grants to deserving students. All applications are reviewed by the Philippine Committee and presented to the Chair of the Scholarship Program of the FEU-NRMF MT International Alumni Society for approval. Grants are provided for students going to Fourth Year and Internship. Four grants of $300 per semester per scholar are available.

Blessed are they who share their blessings, for they shall inherit more! (Anon). Please, fill up the form below to participate in our Scholarship/Awards Program.
